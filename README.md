## Why Symphony AI?

Organizations are racing to integrate AI into their operations — but most face the same challenge: **connecting AI to the systems they already use**. Symphony AI bridges that gap.

Symphony AI is a production-ready framework that brings AI-powered intelligence into your existing enterprise applications — without ripping and replacing what works. Connect your databases, APIs, documents, and workflows to powerful AI models in days, not months.

---

## What We Offer

### AI-Powered Application Framework

Drop Symphony AI into your Java/Spring Boot stack and unlock intelligent capabilities across your entire application landscape:

- **Natural Language Interfaces** — Let users query databases, trigger workflows, and get answers in plain English
- **Multi-Source Intelligence** — Unify data from SQL databases, REST APIs, GraphQL endpoints, and documents into a single AI-driven experience
- **Agentic Workflows** — AI that reasons, plans, and executes multi-step tasks autonomously
- **Document Understanding** — Extract insights from PDFs, Word documents, spreadsheets, and scanned images automatically
- **Smart Email & Notifications** — AI-generated communications with template-driven personalization

### Enterprise AI Consulting

Our team helps you go from concept to production:

- **AI Strategy & Roadmap** — Identify high-impact AI opportunities across your enterprise
- **Custom Integration** — Connect Symphony AI to your existing ERP, CRM, and business systems
- **Knowledge Base Design** — Structure your organizational knowledge for AI-powered retrieval
- **Pilot to Production** — Start small, prove value, then scale across the organization
- **Training & Enablement** — Equip your development team to build and maintain AI-powered features

### AI-as-a-Service (SaaS)

Don't want to host it yourself? We offer managed Symphony AI services:

- **Hosted AI Orchestration** — Fully managed, cloud-hosted Symphony AI instances
- **Pay-Per-Use Pricing** — Scale costs with actual usage, not upfront commitments
- **Multi-Tenant Ready** — Isolated, secure environments for each client or business unit
- **99.9% Uptime SLA** — Enterprise-grade reliability with 24/7 monitoring
- **Automatic Updates** — Always running the latest version with zero downtime

## Built for the Enterprise

- **Java & Spring Boot** — Runs on the stack your team already knows
- **Open Source (MIT License)** — No vendor lock-in, full transparency
- **Available on Maven Central** — Add one dependency and start building
- **Production Hardened** — Caching, session management, connection pooling, and reactive streams built in
- **Extensible by Design** — Custom plugins, steps, and AI providers snap in without modifying core code

## Get Started

Symphony AI is available today on **Maven Central**:

```xml
<dependency>
    <groupId>org.symphonykernel</groupId>
    <artifactId>symphony-ai</artifactId>
    <version>0.1.0</version>
</dependency>
```

Explore the source and documentation on [GitHub](https://github.com/cibinmathewjose/semanticsymphony).
